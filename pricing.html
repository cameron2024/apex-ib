Common questions

Everything you need to know

What's the difference between Monthly and the Recruiting Pass? +
Pro is $19.99/month with 1 mock per 24 hours — good for early-stage prep. The Recruiting Pass is $39.99/month — unlimited mocks, voice mode (speak your answers like the real interview), and early access to new question packs. Both monthly, cancel anytime. If you're doing 2+ mocks per day during active recruiting, the Pass pays for itself in the first week.
How long does the Recruiting Pass last? +
The Recruiting Pass is active from the date of purchase through your full recruiting cycle — typically from summer prep through winter superdays. There's no hard expiry date. You pay once and focus on prep, not on managing a subscription.
Is my progress saved if I downgrade to Free? +
Yes. Your score history, mastery data, and saved questions are all preserved. You just won't be able to generate new AI drill questions or access performance analytics until you upgrade again.
How does the grading actually work? +
Every answer is evaluated by a model built around what investment bankers actually look for in interviews — not generic correctness, but the specific framing, mechanics, and completeness they expect. You get a score from 0–100 across three dimensions — technical accuracy, completeness, and structure — plus specific feedback on what you got right and what you missed.
What makes the AI drill questions different from the regular bank? +
The regular question bank is a fixed set of 565 vetted technical questions. When you score below 75, the model generates 3 completely new questions targeting the specific concept you missed — not a topic, but the exact mechanic (e.g. "how D&A flows through to free cash flow specifically"). These are written fresh for your gap, not pulled from any list.
Is this only for IB? What about PE, consulting, or other finance roles? +
Right now the question bank is focused on investment banking technicals — the core topics (accounting, valuation, DCF, M&A, LBO, enterprise value) that come up in IB and PE first-round interviews. Expansion to other finance roles is on the roadmap.
