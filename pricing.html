Common questions

Everything you need to know

What happens after the 7-day trial? +
After 7 days your Pro trial ends and you're moved to the Free tier automatically — no charge. If you want to keep unlimited grading and AI drills, you can upgrade at any point. No surprise charges.
Is my progress saved if I downgrade to Free? +
Yes. Your score history, mastery data, and saved questions are all preserved. You just won't be able to generate new AI drill questions or access performance analytics until you upgrade again.
How does the AI grading actually work? +
Every answer is graded by Claude against a model answer written by IB professionals. You get a score from 0–100 across three dimensions — technical accuracy, completeness, and structure — plus specific feedback on what you got right and what you missed.
What makes the AI drill questions different from the regular bank? +
The regular question bank is a fixed set of 188 vetted technical questions. When you score below 75, Claude generates 3 completely new questions targeting the specific concept you missed — not a topic, but the exact mechanic (e.g. "how D&A flows through to free cash flow specifically"). These are written fresh for your gap, not pulled from any list.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any point. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period, then roll to Free with no further charges.
Is this only for IB? What about PE, consulting, or other finance roles? +
Right now the question bank is focused on investment banking technicals — the core topics (accounting, valuation, DCF, M&A, LBO, enterprise value) that come up in IB and PE first-round interviews. Expansion to other finance roles is on the roadmap.
