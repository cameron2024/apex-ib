FAQ

Questions before you start

How is the grading actually accurate?
Each question has a rubric — what bankers actually look for in the answer. Apex sends your answer alongside the rubric and a banker-style grading prompt to a frontier AI model, which returns a 0–100 score plus specific feedback. It's calibrated against the same standard senior bankers use mentally when evaluating you across the desk.
Do I need an account to try it?
No — try one question right now, no signup or card required. Guests get 5 graded answers per day. Creating an account is free and adds progress tracking, saved questions, and the time-to-ready estimate.
Is this an alternative to the 400 guide or WSP?
The 400 guide gives you the model answers. WSP teaches you the concepts. Neither tells you if you can deliver the answers in the room. Apex is the missing piece — the active practice and objective feedback that converts knowledge into reps.
Does voice mode work?
Yes — voice mode is available in mock interviews and supported in Chrome and Edge. Your voice is transcribed in-browser and graded the same way as typed answers. Useful for getting the rhythm of speaking your answers out loud, the way you will in the actual interview.
Who built this?
a team of students who've been through IB recruiting firsthand. Apex was built out of that experience — the tool we wished existed when we were prepping. Questions and features ship regularly because we remember exactly what the process demands.
What's the difference between Pro and the Recruiting Pass?
Pro is $19.99/month — unlimited graded answers and 1 mock interview per 24 hours. The Recruiting Pass is $39.99/month — everything in Pro plus unlimited mock interviews, voice mode (speak your answers), and early access to new question packs as they ship. Both are monthly with cancel-anytime. Pick Pro if you're easing into prep; pick the Pass if you're in active recruiting and doing multiple mocks per day.
